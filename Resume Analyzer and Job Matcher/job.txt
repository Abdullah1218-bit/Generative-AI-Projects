Job Title: Data Analyst

Company Overview
We are a growing analytics company looking for a skilled Data Analyst to join our team.

Responsibilities
- Analyze large datasets to extract insights
- Build dashboards and reports
- Work with machine learning models for prediction
- Collaborate with stakeholders to understand business needs

Required Skills
- Python
- SQL
- Data Analysis
- Machine Learning
- Power BI
- Excel
- Pandas

Preferred Skills
- NumPy
- Data Visualization
- Statistics

Experience Required
- Minimum 2 years of experience in data analysis or related field

Education
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Data Science, or related field
